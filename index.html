Our Core Values

At St. Gonzaga Budini Secondary School, our core values guide every aspect of school life, fostering a well-rounded education that nurtures both academic excellence and character development.

Humility/Simplicity

We teach students the importance of being humble and embracing simplicity in life. True greatness lies in serving others selflessly and remaining grounded regardless of achievements.

Integrity/Morally Upright

Integrity is at the heart of everything we do. We emphasize honesty, strong moral principles, and doing what is right, even when no one is watching.

Creativity/Ingenuity

We encourage creative thinking and problem-solving, inspiring students to approach challenges with innovation and original ideas.

Hard Work

Success is earned through dedication and effort. We instill a strong work ethic in our students, encouraging them to persevere and strive for excellence.

Dignity

Every individual is treated with respect and honor. We nurture a school environment where students learn to carry themselves with pride.

Patience and Prayer

Patience and faith are essential to overcoming life's challenges. Through prayer, students are taught to seek spiritual guidance and strength.

Frequently Asked Questions

Below are answers to some of the most frequently asked questions to help guide you as you consider joining our school community.

What are the admission requirements for both O-Level and A-Level students?

Admission to O-Level requires successful completion of Primary Leaving Examinations (PLE) and submission of the results along with other required documents such as a birth certificate and baptism card. For A-Level, students must submit their Uganda Certificate of Education (UCE) results.

Is St. Gonzaga Budini Secondary School a boarding school, and what facilities are available for boarding students?

Yes, St. Gonzaga Budini is a mixed boarding school. We provide separate boarding facilities for boys and girls, along with a safe, nurturing environment. Boarding students benefit from supervised study times, extracurricular activities, and access to facilities like the library, chapel, and sports grounds.

What extracurricular activities are available for students?

Our students can engage in a variety of extracurricular activities, including sports (football, netball, athletics), music, drama, debate, and various clubs such as the Scripture Union, Scouts, Environmental Club, and the Debate Club. These activities help to foster personal development and leadership skills.
